Active ingredients

The formula

Every ingredient chosen for what dog skin actually needs, season after season. Nothing diluted, nothing unnecessary.

01

Colloidal oatmeal

A time-honored skin soother. Fine oat particles form a protective barrier that calms irritation, repairs the skin's natural defenses, and relieves sensitivity year-round.

02

Cocamidopropyl betaine

A gentle, coconut-derived surfactant that cleanses without stripping. Acts as a foam stabilizer for that rich, creamy lather — sulfate-free, skin-kind.

03

Panthenol (pro-vitamin B5)

Penetrates the hair shaft to add lasting softness and shine. Humectant properties help maintain coat moisture and improve manageability through every season.

04

Aloe vera extract

Nature's most versatile calming agent. Rich in polysaccharides, aloe hydrates deeply while soothing dry, itchy, or reactive skin — especially after sun and salt exposure.

05

ONZ-400

A professional-grade odor-neutralizing technology that eliminates rather than masks pet odor at the molecular level — for a genuinely fresh finish that lasts.

06

Glycerin

A deeply effective humectant that draws moisture from the air into the skin. Leaves the coat feeling supple, smooth, and deeply conditioned after every wash.

Our story

Made for Vanilla.
Perfected for every dog.

This started with two dogs and one frustrated bath-time routine. Apple — a spirited golden with sensitive, dry skin — and Vanilla — a fluffy cloud of a dog with a coat that somehow attracted every particle of mud, dust, and general outdoor chaos.

Every product we tried either dried out Apple's skin or left Vanilla's coat dull and smelling faintly of artificial lavender. We wanted something that actually worked — formulated with care, without compromise.

Then we realized the problem wasn't just the formula. It was the approach. Dogs aren't bathed once and forgotten — their skin and coat change with the seasons. So we built a skincare system around that reality. Vanilla & Co. is the first dog brand that thinks the way a skincare brand thinks: routines, not one-offs.
